About Casey

Hey! I'm Casey, a video producer and researcher with a wide-ranging skill set. I grew up in Denver, Colorado and am currently working towards my Master's degree in San Francisco, California.

I developed a passion for storytelling during high school through speech & debate, which led me toward a degree in media production. I wanted to tell interesting stories—so I developed the technical skills to do so. Professionally, I enjoy applying my video production skills in brand strategy and contract work.

After graduating with my bachelor's degree, I sold off all my childhood Pokémon cards (RIP to my Ho-Oh, you served me well) and took some time off to travel across Costa Rica, Nicaragua, and Argentina. I realized there's so much about the world I want to learn, which has led me to my pursuit of a graduate degree in international relations, to understand how both policy and geopolitical dynamics impact individuals, firms, and the world at large.

When I'm not editing videos, behind the camera, or deep in research, you can find me training for my next marathon, weightlifting, reading, or if we're being honest, watching the newest episode of Survivor and eating Taco Bell at my desk. In my work, I value curiosity, collaboration, and the pursuit of mastery.
