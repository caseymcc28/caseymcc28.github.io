About Casey

Hi! I’m Casey, a video editor from Colorado.

I use Adobe software to craft my videos, and am highly adept in Adobe Premiere, After Effects, Photoshop, Illustrator, and Audition. I use DaVinci Resolve to color my videos. Post-production is my favorite kind of work: to me, the narrative comes alive in post, where the pacing, structure, and rhythm of the story comes alive. That being said, I'm also highly familiar with and competent in the overall production process, including camera operation, audio setup, lighting scenes, etc.

My core values in work are curiosity & collaboration. I’m always seeking to learn and grow my skillset, and love working alongside driven and dedicated teammates. When not working, I enjoy traveling, reading, weightlifting, and hiking.
